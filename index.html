Hi, I am Jules Jacobs. I am in the final year of my PhD program at Radboud University, working on formal verification and programming languages under Robbert Krebbers. Additionally, I am investigating deadlock freedom with Stephanie Balzer.

Some of my favourite things are: type theory, parametricity, SSA as a dataflow lattice, generating functions & Kirchoff's matrix-tree theorem, differential forms & the Gauss-Bonnet theorem, star semirings, Lagrangian mechanics.
